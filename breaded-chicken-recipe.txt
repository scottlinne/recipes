Following is my recipe for breaded chicken.
Ingredients:
Chicken Breasts
Olive Oil
Bread Crumbs
Seasoning (Salt, Pepper, other spices of preference)

Cover chicken breasts with Olive Oil
Mix bread crumbs with seasoning Mix
Coat chicken breasts with seasoned bread crumbs in a bowl
Arrange chicken breasts on a pan
Place pan in 350 degree oven for 25 minutes
